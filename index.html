Welcome to Adelaide Pavilion

Where Every Event Becomes a Cherished Memory

Nestled within the lush surrounds of Veale Gardens, Adelaide Pavilion has been the city's most beloved private function venue since 2003. Once home to the legendary Alpine Restaurant, today it stands as an upscale venue where floor-to-ceiling panoramic windows frame garden views, and every detail is curated for elegance.

Our team of dedicated coordinators ensure a seamless, personalised experience — whether you're celebrating your wedding day, hosting a corporate gala, or gathering family and friends for a milestone celebration.
