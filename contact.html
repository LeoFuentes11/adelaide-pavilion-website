FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

We recommend booking as early as possible — popular dates (especially Saturdays from October to April) can book 12–18 months in advance. A deposit is required within 7 business days of invoice to secure your date.
Adelaide Pavilion caters for events from 10 to 300 guests. The Terrace Room suits intimate events, while the Parkview Room is ideal for larger gatherings. Room hire minimums and package minimums may apply — contact us for details.
Veale Gardens offers 13+ stunning outdoor ceremony sites just steps from Adelaide Pavilion. To reserve a ceremony location you'll need to contact Adelaide City Council on (08) 8203 7203. Our team can advise on timing and coordinate a seamless transition from ceremony to reception.
For weddings, BYO spirits are permitted with no corkage (maximum 1 bottle per 10 adult guests). Spirits are not served prior to entrée. Guests are not permitted to bring their own wine, beer, or other beverages — our beverage packages must be used. For social and corporate events, please discuss with our events team.
Absolutely. We cater for all dietary requirements including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, and other allergies. Please advise us of all dietary needs at least 14 business days prior to your event and we will ensure every guest is delighted.
We offer chair hire, chair covers & sashes, ceiling lighting, ceiling draping, backdrops, and stage hire — all available through Adelaide Pavilion's preferred providers (prices on request). External contractors are welcome but must provide a registered ABN and current public liability insurance. Note: glitter, confetti, and streamers incur a cleaning fee of $250.
Deposits are fully non-refundable upon cancellation. If you need to postpone, written permission is required and a new deposit must be paid for the alternative date. If the original date is re-booked at similar value, the first deposit will be refunded less a $500 processing fee. All other monies paid are non-refundable. We strongly recommend event insurance.
Yes — approximately 85 onsite parking bays are available complimentary for your event duration (subject to availability), accessible via the South Terrace paved driveway. Additional parking is available nearby at Gilles Street Auto Park (5 min walk) and Mill Street Car Park (9 min walk).
