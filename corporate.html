Where Business Meets Elegance

Adelaide Pavilion provides an exceptional backdrop for corporate events of all sizes and types. Our two elegantly appointed function rooms, professional AV infrastructure, and outstanding catering combine to create events that impress clients, reward teams, and leave a lasting impression.
