Garden Ceremonies

Ceremony in Veale Gardens

Veale Gardens offers an extraordinary array of outdoor ceremony settings just steps from our doors. From sunken rose gardens to tranquil rock pools, wisteria pergolas to shaded grove lawns — each location is uniquely beautiful and effortlessly romantic.

To reserve a ceremony location within Veale Gardens, contact Adelaide City Council directly on (08) 8203 7203. Our team can advise on timing and logistics to ensure a seamless transition from ceremony to reception.

• Rock Pool 1 & 2 • Hypar • Adelaide Qingdao Rose Garden • Sheoak Grove • Wisteria Pergola • Nurses Memorial Sunken Garden • Eastern Garden • Rose Garden Lawns East
