Our Story

About Adelaide Pavilion

Building est. 1963  ·  Venue est. 2003

Our Heritage

A Living Piece of Adelaide History

Built in the Veale Gardens, the Adelaide Pavilion is housed in a structure with its design based on Cincinnati’s Frisch’s Mainliner Restaurant, noted for its unusual roof design. Although originally named “The Alpine Restaurant” when it first operated in January 1963 because of the Alpine garden established nearby, Adelaide Pavilion is today a private function centre that serves French and Mediterranean inspired cuisine.

The Adelaide Pavilion is a flexible function venue for 10 to 300 guests. The function centre boasts two function spaces with floor to ceiling panoramic windows, natural lighting, spectacular surroundings and immaculate food and service. This multi award-winning venue is able to host and cater for corporate events, social events and wedding receptions. The function centre opened its doors in 2003.

Although strictly a private function centre these days, the Adelaide Pavilion has created a reputation for being customer focused and providing excellent quality produce prepared to the highest standard.

Surrounding the Adelaide Pavilion, Veale Gardens features a lush mixture of evergreen and deciduous trees, a sunken rose garden, works of art, water features and a number of walks. It is a characteristic 1960s-styled semiformal garden, influenced by contemporary European urban public garden designs. The garden is a very popular location for weddings and photography shoots.

Est. 2003

Over Two Decades of Excellence

From its origins as The Alpine Restaurant — housed in a building that has graced Veale Gardens since 1963 — to Adelaide's premier private function venue, Adelaide Pavilion has been creating unforgettable moments since 2003. It remains an exclusively private venue, welcoming weddings, corporate events, and social celebrations with warmth, care, and genuine attention to detail.

Our Spaces

Two Distinct Function Rooms

Each room offers its own unique character, yet both share the Pavilion's signature hallmarks: panoramic garden views, abundant natural light, and an atmosphere of refined elegance.

Function Room One

The Parkview Room

Our grandest and most versatile space, the Parkview Room is a breathtaking setting for large receptions and events. Floor-to-ceiling panoramic windows frame sweeping views of Veale Gardens' lush canopy, flooding the room with natural light and creating an unparalleled connection to nature.

Features include climate-controlled reverse-cycle air conditioning, variable atmospheric lighting, carpet flooring with a parquetry dance floor, and optional partition wall dividers for flexible room sizing.

Function Room Two

The Terrace Room

The Terrace Room is an intimate yet impressive space, distinguished by its soaring 6–7 metre ceilings and a private alfresco terrace area. Panoramic windows with adjustable blinds frame garden views, while the sophisticated grey-tone epoxy flooring adds a contemporary elegance.

Perfectly suited to weddings of up to 90 guests, intimate corporate functions, or exclusive social celebrations, the Terrace Room offers a sense of warmth and privacy within a luxurious setting.

Our Cuisine

French & Mediterranean Inspired

Our culinary philosophy is rooted in the traditions of French and Mediterranean cuisine — fresh, seasonal ingredients transformed into dishes of remarkable depth and elegance.

From creamy potato & leek soup and pork belly with honey balsamic glaze, to salt & pepper calamari, fillet steak with red wine jus, and decadent chocolate mousse — every dish is crafted with care and served with precision.

We proudly cater for all dietary requirements — vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and more — ensuring every guest is delighted.
