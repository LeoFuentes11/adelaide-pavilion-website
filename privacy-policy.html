Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026

Adelaide Pavilion respects your privacy. This page explains what personal information we collect through our website, why we collect it, and what we do with it.

What we collect

When you get in touch through our enquiry form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Function type (wedding, corporate, social, etc.)
  • Estimated number of guests
  • Date of your function
  • How you heard about us
  • Any message or details you include

If your enquiry turns into a booking, we may also collect further details needed to plan your event — for example, dietary requirements or allergy information for catering purposes, and any other event specifics you share with our team.

We don't collect payment card details through the website.

How we use it

We use your information to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and provide a quote
  • Plan and manage your booking if you go ahead
  • Pass relevant details (like dietary requirements) to our kitchen and events staff so your function runs smoothly
  • Understand how people find our website, using the "how did you hear about us" field

We don't sell or trade your information to anyone else.

Who we share it with

We share information only with people who need it to help run your event — our own staff, and, where relevant, contractors like external contractors or hire companies you've asked us to coordinate with. We may also use third-party services to run parts of our website or business. These providers only handle information as needed to provide their service to us.

Cookies and website analytics

Our website may use tools such as Google Analytics or a Facebook pixel to understand how visitors use the site. These tools may use cookies, which you can control through your browser settings.

Storing your information

We take reasonable steps to keep your information secure and only keep it for as long as we need it — generally for as long as it takes to plan and deliver your event, plus a period afterwards for record-keeping (for example, accounting or tax purposes).

Access and correction

You can ask us what personal information we hold about you, or ask us to correct or update it, by contacting us using the details below.

Complaints

If you have a concern about how we've handled your information, please contact us first so we can try to sort it out. If you're not satisfied with our response, you can contact the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top shows when it was last changed.
